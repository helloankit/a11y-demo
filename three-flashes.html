Three Flashes or Below Threshold Demo

This example demonstrates content that flashes at a safe rate (below three flashes per second) in accordance with WCAG 2.1 - SC 2.3.1.

Warning: Flashing content that exceeds three flashes per second can be harmful and may trigger seizures in individuals with photosensitive epilepsy.
These seizures can range from mild to severe, potentially leading to loss of consciousness, convulsions, and physical injury.
